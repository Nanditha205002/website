Our Facilities

At VR International School, we provide state-of-the-art facilities to ensure our students have the best environment to learn and grow.

Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with smart boards, comfortable seating, and ample space to encourage interactive learning.

Laboratories

We have fully equipped science and computer labs that allow students to explore their subjects in depth.

Library

Our library has a vast collection of books, digital resources, and a quiet study area for students.

Sports Facilities

We offer a variety of sports facilities, including a football field, basketball courts, and a swimming pool, to promote physical fitness and teamwork.
