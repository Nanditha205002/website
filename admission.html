Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria

Students must be at least 4 years old for Kindergarten. All applicants must pass an entrance exam.

Steps to Apply

  1. Download the application form from the link below.
  2. Submit the completed form to our admissions office.
  3. Attend the entrance exam and interview.
  4. Receive your admission decision.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
  • Entrance Exam: April 10, 2024
  • Interviews: April 15-20, 2024
  • Results Announcement: May 1, 2024
